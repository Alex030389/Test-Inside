Purchase Process Help

  • Once you've selected a product, select from the available purchase options, such as: Questions And Answer product, Lab Preparations, Study guide and Audio Exam or physical shipment of Hard Copy. Follow the prompts to complete your order. Note: Your steps may vary from these listed here, depending on the page(s) where you started.

    1. Select the product(s) you would like to order and select from the available options such as: Value Packs, Bundles, Hard Copies and click the Add to Cart button.
    2. Your shopping cart will display a list of the product(s) you have ordered. To add more items to your cart Continue Shopping. Once you are finished selecting Products click the Cart button on Top Navigation Bar.
    3. Fill in the Billing Information using the correct Information and click on Pay Now Button to proceed to payment. Before clicking the Pay Now button make sure that all information is Correct. A receipt page displays once you have successfully placed an order. You will then be asked to create login with Test-inside.com. If you currently have a Test-inside account, you may log in through the Log In Now tab and then proceed to Checkout.
    4. Enter your Account Information for the fields (* indicates required fields), and then click the Continue button.
    5. Click the Install from My Account link or the Go to My Account Now button to install your products.

  • Your shopping cart lists the product(s) you have selected, along with quantities and totals. No Taxes are applied to Test-inside Products. You may remove any products from your shopping cart by clicking the "Trash" button in the row of the item you would like to remove. Click the Continue Shopping button to add more items to your shopping cart or the Check Out button once you are done shopping and would like to proceed to checkout.

    Note: After clicking the Continue Shopping button, you can add any other retail product to your cart, you will be redirected to the Test-inside All Products Online Store.

  • We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express and, in some countries, JCB. We also accept PayPal, Moneybookers, Bank Wire Transfer and Western Union.

  • When entering your credit card number on the Billing Information page, do not include spaces or dashes. Once you have completed the required information, click the Pay Now Button to proceed. Please do not click the "Pay Now" button more than once.

  • After checking out, you will be asked to create an account with Test-inside, if you don't already have one. An account is required for accessing the Products.

    Complete the customer profile form and review the privacy policy. Click the Submit Button and your account will be created.

    Remember, the email address used to create your account on Test-inside is your account ID. Use this email address to log in whenever you return to the Test-inside Web site.

    If you currently have a Test-inside Account, you may log in through the Log In Now link.

  • Whenever you purchase a Product, our system begin the 90 day validation period. After Expiry the Products will still show in your login and you can renew them at 40% discount just by click on the "Renew Now" button next to each Expired Product. Your shopping cart will show Product Renewal at 40% Discount. You may now proceed to check out.

  • After Purchase is complete, you will need to download and install your products from Test-inside My Account by clicking on the relevant Download/Install/Update button(s).

  • You can use the Contact Us Page to find the relevant E-mail ID for specific Department.

  • Purchased Test-inside Product can be download twice at any two locations of your choice. Two Authorization codes can be generated for each product from your Test-inside Account. For Reinstalled any previously installed product you may need to reset code. Please contact support@test-inside.com for further assistance.

  • If your request is within the first 30 days of purchase, we'll gladly provide you a full refund. We do not provide partial refunds. Refund can only be claimed in case you have failed corresponding after using Test-inside Questions And Answers Product.

    IF YOU CLAIM FOR REFUND/EXCHANGE EXAM YOU MUST DELETE THE SOFTWARE IF ALREADY INSTALLED.

    You may exercise this right of Product Guarantee by contacting Customer Support at support@test-inside.com

  • To purchase discounted product please Add the Product to cart and use provided Promo code and click Update Button. Contact billing@test-inside.com if problem persists.
